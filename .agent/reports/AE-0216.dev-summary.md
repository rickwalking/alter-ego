## Summary

Built the **smart handoff / smart compact** workflow: capture a long session's
in-flight learnings into a curated checkpoint and auto-re-seed a fresh session,
as a higher-fidelity alternative to repeated `/compact`. Design was shaped by an
architect research pass + an **external cross-LLM cold-critic review**, which
rejected the original fully-automatic-trigger idea as resting on a hook-semantics
misconception. Implemented the robust subset.
