### Summary
The EN carousel render path (`run_bilingual_export` → `slides_data_for_language(slides, "en")` → `run_design`) read `extras.translation_en.heading`/`body` verbatim and rendered them with no sentence-case repair. The existing `_repair_heading_sentence_case_en` / `deterministic_repair_slide_payload` / `repair_localized_slides` only ever operated on the separate `localized_slides` representation — so the actual render source was never sentence-cased (the dual-representation gap the ticket suspected). Added an HTML-tag-aware, idempotent `repair_text_sentence_case_en()` and applied it to the EN heading AND body at the point of consumption inside `slides_data_for_language`, guaranteeing all-lowercase EN copy can no longer reach the renderer.
