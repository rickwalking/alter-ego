SpaceX's $60B bet and what changes

SpaceX secured the right to acquire Cursor for $60B later in 2026, or pay $10B for joint work. The plan: integrate Cursor with Grok and the Colossus supercomputer (200,000 H100 GPUs). This would directly compete with OpenAI's Codex and Anthropic's Claude Code, while giving Cursor access to one of the largest AI clusters in the world.
